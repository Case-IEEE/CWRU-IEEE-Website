Who we are

We are the Case Western Reserve University IEEE Student Chapter. We're a diverse group of students with a passion for learning and solving problems. Our main events are a biweekly tech talk series, Spark Talks, where professors and industry professionals talk about their research, projects, or just an interesting topic. We also host movie nights with other organizations on campus, attend IEEE conferences, run technical workshops, and manage a hardware library.
